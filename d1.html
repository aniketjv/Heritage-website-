How to Reach Raigad Fort

🚗 By Road

  • Mumbai – 170 km (4–5 hrs)
  • Pune – 130 km (3–4 hrs)
  • Nearest village: Pachad

🚆 By Train

  • Nearest station: Mangaon
  • 30 km from Raigad
  • Taxi available

✈ By Air

  • Nearest airport: Mumbai
  • Then travel by road

🥾 Trek & Ropeway

  • 1,700 steps trekking route
  • 1.5–2 hrs climb
  • Ropeway: 4–5 min ride

📌 Travel Tips

  • Carry water
  • Wear trekking shoes
  • Start early morning
  • Best season: Oct – Feb
